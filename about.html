Our History

A decade of healing and growth.

What started as a small two-person clinic has blossomed into a comprehensive mental wellness center serving thousands of individuals and families across the region.

2012 - The Beginning

Founded by Dr. Marcus Thorne and Elena Rodriguez with a mission to make CBT accessible to everyone.

2016 - Expanding Services

Introduced child psychology and family therapy divisions, growing our team to 15 specialists.

2020 - Virtual Care Launch

Rapidly deployed our secure telehealth platform, ensuring uninterrupted care during global challenges.

Today - Stackly Clinic

Now recognized as a regional center of excellence for mental health, serving over 5,000 patients annually.
